About Us

We are

A Yummi place that will remain in your heart for a long time

Our bowls combine a source of carbohydrates (rice or quinoa), a source of protein (traditionally fish or seafood, but we also use chicken), a selection of crisp and colourful veggies, and a dusting of crunchy nuts and sesame, which makes for a meal that's full of essential nutrients.

Of course, portion size, ingredients and cooking technique will impact the caloric and nutritional content of a poke bowl but, generally speaking, poke bowls are a solid choice if you're after a healthy (and tasty) meal.
